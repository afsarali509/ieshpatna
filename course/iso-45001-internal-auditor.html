ISO 45001 Internal Auditor Certification

ISO 45001 Internal Auditor: Occupational Health and Safety Management

Welcome to the ISO 45001 Internal Auditor Course! This specialized training program is designed for professionals who are looking to become certified internal auditors for ISO 45001:2018, the international standard for Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems (OHSMS). Our course provides the essential knowledge and skills needed to conduct effective internal audits, ensuring workplace safety and compliance with ISO 45001 standards.

         COURSE FEE             5,000/- INR
 COURSE DURATION             2 Days
      TRAINING MODE             Online / Offline



 


What is the ISO 45001 Internal Auditor Course?

The ISO 45001 Internal Auditor Course offers a detailed curriculum for individuals who aim to master internal auditing techniques specifically for ISO 45001:2018. This course is ideal for those seeking to advance their careers in occupational health and safety management by developing expertise in conducting internal audits and ensuring OHSMS effectiveness.

Course Overview: What You Will Learn

Our ISO 45001 Internal Auditor Course covers a broad range of topics designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Here’s a detailed breakdown of the course content:

1. Introduction to ISO 45001 and Internal Auditing

Keywords: ISO 45001 Internal Auditor, ISO 45001 Overview, Internal Auditing Basics

  • Understanding ISO 45001: Explore the purpose, scope, and benefits of ISO 45001:2018, which provides a framework for managing occupational health and safety risks and improving workplace safety.
  • Role of the Internal Auditor: Learn about the responsibilities of an ISO 45001 Internal Auditor, including the planning, execution, and reporting of internal audits.
  • Types of Audits: Understand various types of audits, including internal audits, external audits, and certification audits, and the crucial role of internal audits in maintaining an effective OHSMS.

2. Detailed Study of ISO 45001:2018 Requirements

Keywords: ISO 45001:2018 Clauses, ISO 45001 Requirements, Occupational Health and Safety Standards

  • ISO 45001 Clauses: Delve into the main clauses of ISO 45001:2018, such as the context of the organization, leadership, planning, support, operation, performance evaluation, and improvement.
  • Interpreting ISO 45001 Standards: Learn how to interpret and apply ISO 45001 requirements to real-world OHSMS scenarios.
  • Documentation and Records: Understand the documentation requirements for ISO 45001, including the creation of safety policies, objectives, procedures, and maintaining records for OHSMS compliance.

3. Planning and Preparing for Internal Audits

Keywords: Internal Audit Planning, ISO 45001 Audit Preparation, Audit Planning Techniques

  • Audit Planning: Learn to develop an effective internal audit plan, including setting clear audit objectives, defining the scope, criteria, and selecting audit team members.
  • Preparation Techniques: Study techniques for preparing for internal audits, such as reviewing OHSMS documentation, assessing risks, and creating detailed audit checklists.
  • Pre-Audit Activities: Explore essential pre-audit activities including gathering background information, reviewing previous audit reports, and organizing initial meetings.

4. Conducting Internal Audits

Keywords: Conducting ISO 45001 Internal Audits, Internal Audit Techniques, Auditor Skills

  • Executing the Audit: Learn the key steps for conducting an ISO 45001 internal audit, including initiating the audit, interviewing employees, observing processes, and collecting evidence.
  • Effective Auditing Techniques: Study techniques for effective internal auditing, including how to ask the right questions, gather evidence, and observe processes.
  • Managing the Audit Process: Develop skills for managing the audit process, including maintaining audit schedules, ensuring compliance with ISO 45001, and addressing issues during the audit.

5. Reporting Audit Findings and Follow-Up Actions

Keywords: ISO 45001 Audit Reporting, Internal Auditor Reporting, Audit Findings Documentation

  • Documenting Findings: Learn to document audit findings, prepare non-conformance reports, and draft audit summaries that effectively communicate results.
  • Reporting Procedures: Understand the procedures for reporting audit findings to management and relevant stakeholders.
  • Follow-Up Actions: Study methods for verifying corrective actions, ensuring that audit findings are addressed, and maintaining OHSMS compliance.

6. Advanced Auditing Techniques for ISO 45001

Keywords: Advanced ISO 45001 Auditing Techniques, Internal Auditor Skills, Advanced Audit Practices

  • Advanced Techniques: Explore advanced auditing techniques such as risk-based auditing, root cause analysis, and strategic audit planning.
  • Case Studies: Engage with real-world case studies to apply advanced techniques and address complex occupational health and safety challenges.
  • Best Practices: Discover best practices for conducting effective internal audits and ensuring that OHSMS is both efficient and compliant with ISO 45001 standards.

7. ISO 45001 and Integration with Other Management Systems

Keywords: IMS and ISO 45001, Integrated Management Systems, ISO 45001 Integration

  • IMS Concepts: Learn about Integrated Management Systems (IMS) and how ISO 45001 integrates with other standards such as ISO 9001 (Quality Management) and ISO 14001 (Environmental Management).
  • Audit Integration: Study strategies for integrating audits across multiple management systems to streamline processes and enhance overall organizational effectiveness.

8. ISO 45001 Internal Auditor Certification

Keywords: ISO 45001 Internal Auditor Certification, Lead Auditor Exam Preparation, Certification Process

  • Certification Process: Understand the steps to achieve ISO 45001 Internal Auditor certification, including examination requirements and the role of certification bodies.
  • Exam Preparation: Receive guidance on preparing for the ISO 45001 Internal Auditor certification exam, including study resources, practice exams, and exam strategies.
  • Career Development: Explore career opportunities for certified ISO 45001 Internal Auditors and pathways for professional growth in occupational health and safety management.

9. Ethics and Professional Standards in Internal Auditing

Keywords: Auditing Ethics, ISO 45001 Professional Standards, Internal Auditor Ethics

  • Ethical Standards: Study the ethical standards and professional conduct expected of ISO 45001 Internal Auditors, including principles of integrity, objectivity, and confidentiality.
  • Handling Ethical Issues: Learn techniques for managing ethical dilemmas and maintaining high professional standards during audits.
  • Professionalism in Auditing: Develop a professional approach to internal auditing that aligns with ISO 45001 standards and industry best practices.

10. Practical Exercises and Mock Exams

Keywords: ISO 45001 Internal Auditor Exam, Practical Auditing Exercises, Mock Internal Audits

  • Practical Exercises: Participate in hands-on exercises to practice auditing techniques, review OHSMS documentation, and conduct mock internal audits.
  • Mock Exams: Engage in mock exams to assess your knowledge and readiness for the ISO 45001 Internal Auditor certification exam.
  • Study Resources: Access a range of study materials and resources to support your preparation for the certification exam and enhance your learning experience.
