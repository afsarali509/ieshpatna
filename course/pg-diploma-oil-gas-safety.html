PG Diploma In Oil & Gas Safety Management

PG Diploma In Oil & Gas Safety Management

PG Diploma In Oil & Gas Safety Management is Certified Safety Professional Course (CSPD-OG) which provides a systematic development of skills and knowledge of Oil & Gas Safety Managementwith ILO, OSHA, international Oil & Gas Safety standards and Occupational health and safety legislations.

         COURSE FEE             15,000/- INR
 COURSE DURATION             1 Year (45 Days in Fast track Mode )
      TRAINING MODE             Online / Regular / Fast track / Offline



 


Postgraduate Diploma in Oil & Gas Safety Management

Description: The Postgraduate Diploma in Oil & Gas Safety Management is a specialized program designed to equip individuals with advanced knowledge and skills in ensuring safety within the oil and gas industry. This program covers a comprehensive range of topics related to safety regulations, risk assessment, emergency response, and best practices specific to the oil and gas sector.

Course Outline:

Introduction to Oil & Gas Safety Management

  • Understanding the Unique Safety Challenges in the Oil & Gas Industry
  • Role of Safety Managers and Officers in Oil & Gas
  • Historical Incidents and Lessons Learned
  • Legal and Regulatory Framework for Oil & Gas Safety

Oil & Gas Regulations and Standards

  • Industry-specific Safety Regulations (e.g., OSHA, API, IADC)
  • International Safety Management Codes (ISM Code)
  • Regulatory Agencies and Authorities
  • Compliance Audits and Inspections in Oil & Gas Operations

Risk Assessment and Hazard Management in Oil & Gas

  • Identifying and Assessing Hazards in Oil & Gas Operations
  • Hierarchy of Controls for Risk Mitigation
  • Job Hazard Analysis (JHA) and Job Safety Analysis (JSA)
  • Safety Measures for High-Risk Activities (e.g., Drilling, Well Control)

Process Safety Management in Oil & Gas

  • Understanding Process Safety and Its Importance
  • Process Safety Regulations (e.g., API 750, API 521)
  • Layers of Protection Analysis (LOPA) and Risk Assessment
  • Emergency Shutdown Systems and Relief Valve Design

Fire and Explosion Hazards in Oil & Gas

  • Identifying Fire and Explosion Hazards in Oil & Gas Facilities
  • Fire Protection Systems for Oil & Gas Operations
  • Explosion Prevention Measures and Control Systems
  • Case Studies of Fire and Explosion Incidents

Emergency Response Planning and Management

  • Developing Effective Emergency Response Plans for Oil & Gas
  • Incident Command System (ICS) in Oil & Gas Emergencies
  • Crisis Communication Strategies
  • Oil Spill Response and Cleanup Techniques

Offshore and Onshore Safety Considerations

  • Safety Challenges in Offshore Oil & Gas Operations
  • Marine and Helicopter Safety Procedures
  • Onshore Facility Safety and Security Measures
  • Safe Work Practices for Remote Locations

Health and Occupational Hazards in Oil & Gas

  • Health Risks and Occupational Hazards in Oil & Gas Operations
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) for Oil & Gas Workers
  • Monitoring and Managing Hazardous Substances (e.g., H2S)
  • Ergonomics and Mental Health in Oil & Gas Settings

Construction Safety in Oil & Gas Projects

  • Construction Safety Planning and Coordination
  • Hazards in Oil & Gas Construction Projects
  • Managing Safety During Equipment Installation and Commissioning
  • Safety Measures for Major Plant Shutdowns

Safety Management Systems in Oil & Gas

  • Implementing Safety Management Systems (SMS)
  • ISO 45001: Occupational Health & Safety Management Systems
  • Continuous Improvement and Auditing of SMS
  • Integrating SMS with Operational Procedures

Environmental Protection and Sustainability in Oil & Gas

  • Environmental Regulations and Considerations in Oil & Gas
  • Preventing Oil Spills and Contamination
  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) for Oil & Gas Projects
  • Green Initiatives and Sustainable Practices in the Industry

Incident Investigation and Root Cause Analysis

  • Conducting Effective Incident Investigations
  • Root Cause Analysis Techniques
  • Learning from Near Misses and Incidents
  • Preventing Recurrence of Similar Events

Security Measures for Oil & Gas Facilities

  • Physical Security and Access Control
  • Cybersecurity in Oil & Gas Operations
  • Insider Threat Prevention and Mitigation
  • Security Planning and Crisis Management

Emerging Technologies in Oil & Gas Safety

  • Remote Monitoring and Sensing Technologies
  • Digital Twin Applications for Safety Management
  • Robotics and Drones for Inspection and Maintenance
  • Predictive Analytics for Risk Assessment

Capstone Project: Comprehensive Oil & Gas Safety Plan

  • Developing an Integrated Safety Plan for Oil & Gas Operations
  • Incorporating Risk Assessment, Emergency Response, and Process Safety Measures
  • Presentation and Evaluation of Capstone Projects

Assessment Methods:

  • Quizzes and Examinations
  • Case Studies and Hazard Assessments
  • Process Safety Management Projects
  • Capstone Project Proposal and Presentation
  • Practical Demonstrations and Simulations

Graduates of this Postgraduate Diploma program will be equipped with the expertise to lead safety efforts within the oil and gas industry, manage risks, and ensure the well-being of workers, facilities, and the environment. They will play a crucial role in maintaining the highest safety standards in one of the world's most critical and challenging industries
