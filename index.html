About
IEHS Academy

IEHS Academy is a Globally recognized Autonomous International Quality Safety Research Council, which offers internal and external training courses in the field of Environment, Health -Safety &Quality. IEHS Academy adopted & liberalized EHS Training from the International Conventions and recommendations by ILO (International Labour Organization)

IEHS Academy also adopted Safety regulations from OSHA USA, to promote International Labour Standards on Occupational Safety and Health.
